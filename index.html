FAQs

What kind of AI/ML projects do you handle?

I specialize in developing AI/ML solutions across diverse domains, including predictive analytics, computer vision, recommendation systems, and data-driven decision support.

I focus on building end-to-end pipelines covering data preprocessing, model development, evaluation, and deployment.

I leverage scikit-learn, XGBoost, OpenCV, Hugging Face Transformers, and cloud services such as Azure ML and Google Cloud AI to create scalable, production-ready AI solutions that integrate smoothly with web and enterprise applications.

Can you tell us about your experience in developing AI Voice Agent systems?

I specialize in developing AI Voice Agent systems for customer support, virtual assistants, and conversational interfaces. I work on natural language understanding, speech recognition, real-time communication, and API integration.

I mainly use Retell AI for language processing, Vapi for seamless API connectivity, and Twilio for telephony and real-time communication, combining AI/ML expertise with full-stack development to deliver scalable, production-ready voice solutions.

Can you tell us about your experience in developing AI automation systems?

I specialize in building AI automation systems for business process optimization, predictive analytics, and intelligent workflow management. I work on data preprocessing, model integration, real-time predictions, and automated task execution.

I mainly use N8N, Zapier, and Make.com to design end-to-end automated workflows, combined with AI/ML models built in Python, PyTorch, and TensorFlow, delivering scalable, efficient, and production-ready automation solutions.

Can you tell us about your recent projects?

Recently, I developed an AI-powered Restaurant Reservation System that allowed customers to book tables seamlessly through voice conversations. The main challenge was enabling the AI to handle natural, human-like dialogue while accurately managing booking details in real time.

To achieve this, I used Retell AI as the core engine for speech recognition and natural language understanding. This allowed the system to interpret customer requests—such as reservation times, party sizes, or special dining needs—and respond instantly in a conversational manner. I also integrated the agent with the restaurant’s backend system so it could check table availability, confirm reservations, and send real-time updates automatically.

The solution was built with scalability and reliability in mind, ensuring consistent performance even during peak dining hours. By leveraging Retell AI’s capabilities, I was able to create a voice assistant that feels intuitive, reduces the need for manual staff intervention, and improves the overall customer experience.
